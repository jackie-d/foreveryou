From an useful idea to a real web-app

ForeverYou is a web-app developed by Jackie D as a consequence of life experience and personal growth also during a path with a big company for personal improvement.

All the code is developed and written by the coder who has more than 10 years of professional experience for many projects and companies and it follows the most modern practices and using the latest technologies.

The back-end environment where the data are stored on the server for functioning is provided by Google Firebase, a modern SaaS that's very fast and very secure.

This web-app is freely published for the use and it tries to help people to achieve a better experience of messaging use in some specific situation of daily life that may be sometime challenging.

The developer itself is a person who traveled in more than 30 countries worldwide and lately lived in homeland in the U.S.

The web-app is developed in open-source and it is accepted feedback and opinion from the community to try to make the product always better and most easy and secure to use.
